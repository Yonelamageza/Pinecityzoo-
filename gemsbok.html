Gemsboks

Gemsbok, (Oryx gazella), species of oryx, or large antelope, that lives in desert regions of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

Gemsbok are the largest species of oryx and are powerfully built, with sturdy, muscular bodies

They are known in particular for their striking coloration, being gray-brown with contrasting black and white markings on the face and legs and a long black tail.

They also have remarkable horns, which are long, straight, and backward-pointing.
