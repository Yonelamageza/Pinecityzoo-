Lost Forest

Explore the enchanting Lost Forest, a lush area filled with exotic plants and hidden paths.

Wander through dense foliage and discover unique flora and fauna that thrive in this secluded environment.

The Lost Forest is home to various species of birds, insects, and small mammals, making it a biodiversity hotspot.

Visitors can enjoy guided tours to learn about the ecosystem and conservation efforts in place to protect this natural habitat.

Don't forget to bring your camera to capture the stunning scenery and wildlife encounters.

Whether you're a nature enthusiast or just looking for a peaceful retreat, the Lost Forest offers a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of the zoo.

Take your time to explore the winding trails and immerse yourself in the tranquility of this hidden gem.

Remember to respect the environment and leave no trace as you enjoy your adventure in the Lost Forest.
